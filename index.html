Voila Bot Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how our bot collects, uses, and discloses information about users when you interact with our bot on Discord.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • User Messages: To perform administrative tasks such as ban, kick, mute, unmute, voicemute, voiceunmute, and clear messages.
  • User IDs and Usernames: To identify and manage users for administrative actions.
  • Guild Information: Such as server ID, channel IDs, and roles to facilitate bot functions.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Execute administrative commands as requested by users.
  • Moderate content and ensure compliance with server rules.
  • Provide responses and interact with users effectively.

Information Sharing

We do not share your information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or to comply with legal processes.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users or others.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain user data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Data related to user messages and administrative actions are stored temporarily and cleared regularly.
